About me

My research interests in the area of surgical robotics are continuum robots, robotic palpation, and haptics for surgical robotics. My work focuses on task-based design and modeling of the tele-surgical robotic systems to aid in determining the best suitable manipulator design for a given task, continuum robots, and robotic assistance for trauma care. I am interested in being involved and keep contributing to the field of surgical robotics.

📍 Born and brought up in Surat. Currently living in the city of music and home to the Vanderbilt University, Nashville.

In 2022, spent four months as a mechanical engineering intern with the research and innovation team at Johnson & Johnson, MedTech.
