### More Information
I am a PhD student working in the department of Mechanical Engineering at Vanderbilt University and work as a graduate research assistant at [ARMA Lab](http://arma.vuse.vanderbilt.edu/), advised by Dr. Nabil Simaan. The broad research categories that I am interestied in are surgical robotics, robotic palpation, and haptics for surgical robotics. My current research is in design and modeling of assitive robotics for surgical confined spaces and remote interventions.

Prior to graduate school, I worked as a project research assistant at IIT Bombay with Dr. Azizuddin Khan on developing multisensory application for aiding kids with dyslexia in learning English alphabets and words. I also worked as a junior research fellow at IIT Bombay with Dr. Abhishek Gupta on developing powered transtibial prosthesis.

I received my Bachelor of Technology in Mechanical Engineering from [NIT Surat](https://www.svnit.ac.in/). You can check out my past experiences and corresponding project reports on my Linked-In profile.
